Step 2: Assign the E5 License to the Group

1. In Admin Center https://admin.microsoft.com, go to Billing > Licensing.

2. Select Microsoft 365 E5.

3. Click Groups

4. Click + Assign licenses.

5. Search and choose your new group (Lic_365_E5).

6. Select the services you want (e.g., Windows 11 Enterprise, Defender, Purview).

7. Click Assign.
